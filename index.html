About Me

I'm Dan and I'm a
UX/UI Designer

In September 2020, I left my job as a personal trainer to pursue a career in Software Development. To do this I enrolled on The School of Code's intensive 16 week bootcamp, which took me from a complete novice, to a developer confident in JavaScript, HTML and CSS. I am also competent in using Figma, Jira and Balsamiq, to plan and design products. Throughout the course, I discovered my passion for UX/UI design. To me, it is the more human side of development and I find it facinating. I love researching into how people interact with different technologies and designing based on this research and not just on the assumptions I have.
Throughout the course, we completed many varied projects as pairs and in larger teams. The entire course was remote, so I'm no stranger to remote learning and working as part of an Agile team.
Please feel free to view my CV and explore some of the work I completed during the bootcamp and since finishing.
